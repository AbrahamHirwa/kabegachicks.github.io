KabegaChicks LTD is based in Kigali, Rwanda. We import high-quality day-old broiler chicks from trusted farms in Europe and supply them to Rwandan businesses and poultry farmers.

We believe in supporting food production by providing strong, healthy, fast-growing chicks to people who raise poultry for meat.
